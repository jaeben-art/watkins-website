Terms & Conditions

Welcome to Watkins Shared Adventures ("WSA"). The following Terms & Conditions outline important information regarding our group travel experiences, booking policies, traveler expectations, and operational procedures.

These terms apply to all trips organized by Watkins Shared Adventures in partnership with Diamond Tours.

Our Role

Watkins Shared Adventures organizes group travel experiences in partnership with Diamond Tours, the tour operator responsible for transportation, lodging, itinerary planning, and attraction arrangements.

WSA serves as the group organizer and booking facilitator. While we coordinate communication and provide leadership throughout the trip, we do not own or operate transportation companies, hotels, attractions, or other third-party service providers.

Traveler Conduct & Responsibilities

We are committed to creating a welcoming, enjoyable, and respectful travel experience for all participants.

Travelers are responsible for reviewing itinerary details, departure times, and travel requirements prior to travel. Travelers are also responsible for providing any required identification, including passports for international trips.

All travelers are expected to conduct themselves in a courteous, respectful, and cooperative manner at all times.

Travelers agree to:

  • Treat fellow travelers, WSA leaders, tour staff, drivers, hotel staff, and attraction personnel with courtesy and respect
  • Follow instructions and adhere to scheduled departure times
  • Use personal electronic devices in a manner that does not disturb others (earbuds or headphones are strongly encouraged)
  • Be mindful of shared spaces, including limiting excessive noise
  • Refrain from disruptive, aggressive, abusive, or inappropriate behavior
  • Avoid excessive intoxication that negatively impacts the group
  • Respect motorcoach property, hotel property, and attraction facilities

WSA reserves the right to request reasonable health-related precautions from any traveler exhibiting visible symptoms of illness in order to maintain a safe and comfortable group environment. Such precautions may include wearing a face covering or maintaining appropriate distance when feasible.

Failure to comply with reasonable instructions from WSA may result in removal from the trip without refund. Any expenses incurred as a result of removal, including lodging or transportation home, are the responsibility of the traveler.

WSA also reserves the right to decline future bookings from individuals whose conduct has negatively impacted prior trips.

Payments

Deposit

A $75 per person deposit is required to secure a reservation.

Final Payment

The remaining balance is due approximately two (2) months prior to departure, as specified for each trip.

Failure to submit final payment by the stated due date may result in cancellation of the reservation. If you are experiencing difficulty getting your payment sent, please contact us.

Cancellations & Reservation Adjustments

Standard Cancellation

Cancellations made at least two (2) weeks prior to departure are eligible for a full refund, including the deposit, provided WSA is notified in sufficient time to process the cancellation with Diamond Tours.

Cancellation date is determined by the date WSA receives and acknowledges the cancellation request.

Late Cancellation (Within Two Weeks of Departure)

Cancellations made less than two (2) weeks before departure are not eligible for a refund under Diamond Tours' policy.

Occupancy Adjustments

If one traveler cancels from a Double or Triple Occupancy reservation, the remaining traveler(s) are responsible for any price adjustment resulting from the occupancy change.

Travel Insurance

Diamond Tours works with Travel Confident to provide optional travel insurance coverage.

Purchase of travel insurance is entirely optional and is at the sole discretion of the traveler. Insurance must be purchased by the traveler online through Travel Confident at www.travelconfident.com.

The cost of insurance varies based on the cost of the trip. Please check the website and enter your tour number to check rates. The tour numbers are available at the bottom of the Trips page.

WSA's role is limited to providing information about the availability of travel insurance. WSA is not responsible for insurance policy administration, coverage decisions, claim determinations, or disputes. All questions and claims must be directed to Travel Confident.

Watkins Shared Adventures Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

Watkins Shared Adventures ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application (the "App") collects, uses, and safeguards information when you use it.

1. Information We Collect

a. Information You Provide

Depending on how you use the App, we may collect:

  • Trip unlock codes you enter to access trip details
  • Chat messages you send in trip group chat
  • Display name (if you sign in or participate in chat)
  • Contact information (only if you submit it through a contact/inquiry form)

You are not required to create an account to view most trip information.

b. Optional Sign-In Information

Some features (such as chat participation and message attribution) may use optional sign-in, including Apple Sign In and Google Sign In.

If you sign in, we may receive:

  • A unique user identifier
  • A display name (if provided)
  • An email address (only if you choose to share it)

We do not collect or access your password.

c. Automatically Collected Information

We may collect limited technical data needed for app functionality, including:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version
  • Anonymous identifiers used for caching/syncing
  • Diagnostic data for crash reporting and performance monitoring

This data is generally not used to directly identify you.

2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Display trip itineraries, schedules, and updates
  • Enable group chat and message attribution
  • Send notifications related to trips you have unlocked
  • Improve app performance, reliability, and safety
  • Respond to support requests and inquiries

We do not sell or rent your personal information.

3. Data Storage and Security

Trip content and app data are stored using secure cloud services. We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we follow industry-standard practices to reduce risk.

4. How We Share Information

We do not share personal information with third parties except:

  • With service providers needed to operate the App (such as cloud hosting, authentication, messaging, and notification providers)
  • When required by law, legal process, or to protect rights and safety
  • With other authorized users in the same trip chat (chat content is visible to participants in that trip)

5. Notifications

If you enable notifications, we may send:

  • Trip updates
  • Leader messages
  • Important announcements

You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Your Choices

You may:

  • Use most of the App without signing in
  • Disable notifications
  • Stop using the App at any time

If you have signed in and want to remove participation from a trip chat, you may do so using available app controls.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be reflected by the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the App after updates means you accept the revised policy.

Shared Adventures, Lasting Memories

Watkins Shared Adventures provides exciting, comfortable, and highly social motorcoach journeys across North America.

We specialize in orchestrating large-group travel, bringing together 30 to 50 travelers per itinerary to explore the best destinations across the United States, Canada, and Mexico. Our tours are professionally coordinated in partnership with Diamond Tours, ensuring seamless logistics, quality accommodations, and exceptional value on every trip.

We encourage couples, friends, and small groups to sign up together, where they are welcomed into a larger community of fun-loving travelers. Our adventures are specifically designed to foster connections, allowing you to relax, sightsee, and meet new friends for a truly shared experience.

If you're ready to trade in the stress of solo planning for the joy of a fully-guided adventure with a ready-made community, Watkins Shared Adventures is your ideal travel partner.
